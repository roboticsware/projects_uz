# Let's build a game!

In this project, you will learn how to make a simple sports game in Scratch.

## Step 1: Draw the pitch
Start by creating a green backdrop for your football pitch.

## Step 2: Add the characters
Add a Cat sprite as the player and a Bear sprite as the goalie.

## Step 3: Game logic
Add some Scratch blocks to make the goalie move left and right!

---
